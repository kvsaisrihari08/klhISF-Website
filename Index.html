About Us

The IETE Student Forum (ISF) is a vibrant platform dedicated to fostering technical knowledge, innovation, and professional development among students in the fields of electronics, telecommunications, and information technology. As an integral part of the Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers (IETE), ISF aims to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry demands.
Our forum encourages collaboration, learning, and leadership through various activities such as technical workshops, seminars, competitions, guest lectures, and industrial visits. ISF provides students with a unique opportunity to engage with experts, stay updated with emerging technologies, and develop essential skills to excel in their careers.
We believe in empowering students to become future-ready professionals and innovators by promoting a culture of continuous learning and creativity. Join us to connect, learn, and grow as a community passionate about technology and engineering excellence.
